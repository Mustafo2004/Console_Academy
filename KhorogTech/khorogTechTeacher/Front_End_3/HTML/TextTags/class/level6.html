Plan Your Weekend Getaway

Friday Evening

  1. Pack essentials (clothes, toiletries)
  2. Prepare snacks and drinks for the trip
  3. Check car condition (fuel, tire pressure)
  4. Set GPS directions to destination

Saturday Activities

  • Arrive at destination by noon
  • Check into accommodation
  • Explore local attractions:
    • Visit historical sites
    • Try local cuisine
    • Go for a nature hike
  • Relax by the beach or pool

Sunday Schedule

  1. Enjoy breakfast at a local cafe
  2. Participate in a guided tour or activity
  3. Shop for souvenirs
  4. Depart for home by late afternoon

Planning these activities will make your weekend getaway memorable and enjoyable!
