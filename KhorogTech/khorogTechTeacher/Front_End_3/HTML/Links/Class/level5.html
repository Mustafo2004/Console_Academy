Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain a healthy eating habit:

  1. Stay Hydrated
  2. Eat a Balanced Diet
  3. Watch Your Portions
Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water is crucial for your health. Aim to drink at least 8 glasses of water a day to stay hydrated.

Eat a Balanced Diet

Ensure your diet includes a variety of foods from all food groups: fruits, vegetables, proteins, grains, and dairy. This will provide your body with essential nutrients.

Watch Your Portions

Be mindful of your portion sizes to avoid overeating. Using smaller plates and bowls can help control portion sizes.
